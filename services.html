Modular Solutions for Wicked Problems

My services are modular, allowing you to partner with me for a single phase or the entire journey. I bridge the gap between your abstract strategy and concrete execution.

Phase 1

Vision & Strategy

(The Diagnosis)

Focus

Getting the "Fuzzy" problem sharp.

How I Support You

Through deep-dive sessions, value analysis, and context mapping, I help you find calm in the chaos.

The Result

A clear problem definition, a supported vision, and a strategic direction.

Phase 2

Design

(The Structure)

Focus

Structuring the solution.

What I do

I select the right tools for the job. This involves stakeholder analysis, design sprints, and iterative sketching to translate strategy into requirements.

The Result

A Design Brief, functional design, or blueprint for your solution.

Phase 3

Create & Build

(The Creation)

Focus

Making the solution tangible.

What I do

I don't just advise; I build. From prototyping and tool creation to writing strategic documents and visualizations.

The Result

A Proof of Concept (PoC), Prototype, or MVP that works and adds immediate value.

Our Philosophy

I work with you, not just for you. I believe the solution often already exists within your organization's latent knowledge. By listening and co-creating, I ensure the solution is understood and embraced by the people who will use it.
